The Arabian Nights

In the bustling town of Agrabah in the Middle East, Princess Jasmine feels confined by her father's efforts to arrange a royal marriage for her. Meanwhile, Jafar, the Sultan's trusted advisor, schemes to seize the throne for himself. When Jasmine ventures out of the palace in disguise, she immediately connects with Aladdin, a charming street urchin.
